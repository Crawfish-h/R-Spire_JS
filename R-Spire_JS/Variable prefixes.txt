R[...]: RObject is the root parent class of all classes that can exist without being attached to another object.
G[...]: Variables will have this prefix when extended from RGame_Object.
T[...]: Simple classes/types that do not extend from RGame_Object or RObject. These usually have few functions and contain mostly variables.
F[...]: Complex classes/types that do not extend from RGame_Object or RObject. These must have many functions.
M[...]: miscellaneous files.
